Data Deletion Instructions

If you would like to request the deletion of your data, please follow the instructions below:

  1. Contact our support team at LenDaFriend Team
  2. Include "Data Deletion Request" in the subject line of your email.
  3. Provide the necessary information to verify your identity.
  4. Our support team will process your request and delete your data accordingly.
